My LaTeX Macros

I write everything in LaTeX. You may find some of my macros helpful. Use and edit these at your own risk. However, if you find any side effect, please don't hesitate to send me an email.

For those who don't know what a macro is (yes, I was those), put my whatevermacro.sty in the same folder with your whatsoever.tex file. Now you can import it with the \usepackage{whatevermacro} command.
